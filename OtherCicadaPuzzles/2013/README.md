The second Cicada 3301 puzzle began on January 4, 2013, exactly one year after the first.

## Overview

The 2013 puzzle built on the 2012 format with more complex challenges:
- More sophisticated steganography
- Multiple TOR hidden services
- References to The Book of the Law (Aleister Crowley)
- Twitter announcements
- Physical posters in more locations

---

## Puzzle Stages

### Stage 1: Image & Initial Clues
- New image posted with steganography
- Multiple decryption layers
- Led to TOR onion addresses

### Stage 2: The Book of the Law
- Cipher references to Crowley's work
- Gematria calculations
- Hidden messages in occult texts

### Stage 3: Onion Sites
- Multiple TOR hidden services
- Progressive unlocking mechanism
- Encrypted challenges

### Stage 4: Selection
- Winners received private communications
- Recruitment into unknown organization
