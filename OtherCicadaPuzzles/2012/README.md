## Overview

The 2012 puzzle was a multi-stage cryptographic challenge that included:
- Steganography (hidden data in images)
- Caesar ciphers
- Book ciphers referencing Agrippa by William Gibson
- Physical locations with QR codes
- MIDI audio files
- TOR hidden services

---

## Puzzle Stages

### Stage 1: The Original Image
- Posted on 4chan /b/
- Contains OutGuess steganography
- Hidden message led to Caesar cipher

### Stage 2: Subreddit & Book Cipher
- Reddit clue: r/a2e7j6ic78h0j
- Book cipher using "Agrippa" by William Gibson
- Led to TOR hidden service

### Stage 3: GPS Coordinates
- Physical locations in 14 countries
- Posters with QR codes
- Phone numbers to call

### Stage 4: Email & Selection
- Winners received encrypted emails
- Final recruitment phase
- Approximately 20-30 winners selected
