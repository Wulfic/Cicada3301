## Thematic Analysis

### Key Concepts

1. **Instar**
   - A stage in the development of insects (particularly cicadas) between molts
   - Cicadas spend years underground as nymphs before emerging
   - Symbolizes transformation and hidden development

2. **Tunneling to the Surface**
   - The cicada nymph digs upward through soil
   - Represents striving toward enlightenment/revelation
   - The journey from darkness (ignorance) to light (knowledge)

3. **Shed Our Own Circumferences**
   - Circumference = boundaries, limitations
   - Like the cicada shedding its exoskeleton
   - Releasing self-imposed mental/spiritual constraints

4. **Find the Divinity Within**
   - Inner enlightenment, not external authority
   - Gnostic/mystical theme of divine spark
   - Self-realization and awakening

5. **Emerge**
   - Transformation complete
   - The adult cicada emerging from underground
   - Spiritual rebirth/awakening
